Our Story

The Taste Behind Food Frame

In the heart of Dhaka, 2025 marked the beginning of a flavorful journey called Food Frame Restaurant — a dream born from love for food and the passion to bring people together through taste.

It all started with a simple idea:
"Every meal deserves a perfect frame."

Our founders wanted to create more than just a restaurant — they wanted to build a place where Bangladeshi tradition meets modern taste, where every dish is crafted like a piece of art.

From the aromatic Kacchi Biryani to the sizzling Fiery Chicken Burger, every plate carries a story — a story of home, spice, and happiness.

Food Frame isn’t just about eating; it’s about experiencing food. Whether it’s the laughter of friends over crispy fuchka, a family dinner with kala bhuna, or a quiet moment with iced lemon mint — every moment becomes a memory.

Today, Food Frame stands proudly as a symbol of taste, quality, and creativity — serving flavors that define Bangladesh. Food Frame Restaurant — Every flavor tells a story.
